⚾ 2025 SEASON MIGRATION

What this does

Adds the 2025 season to the app: each pitcher's final leaderboard stats (execution % and velocities) plus their video links. 2025 was recorded as season totals — not pitch-by-pitch — so it powers the leaderboard, the year-to-year trend charts, and the season table, but it has no command heat map or pitch-location map (there's no 2025 zone data). This does not touch your 2026 data or change which season is "current."

Step 1 · Get the data from the 2025 sheet

  1. Open the 2025 spreadsheet → Extensions → Apps Script
  2. Paste in the export-2025.gs script, then run exportSeason2025
  3. Open the execution log, copy everything between the BEGIN / END markers
  4. Paste it in the box below
